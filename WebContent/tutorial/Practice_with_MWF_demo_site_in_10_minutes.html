Practice with MWF Demo Site

  1. Register your account;
  2. Get a workflow template;
  3. Start a workflow process;
  4. Check work list;
  5. Do task;
  6. Repeat step 4 to 5 until the workflow process end.

1.Register your account;

Visit http://www.myworldflow.com/cflow/reg.jsp And login here http://www.myworldflow.com/cflow/login.jsp

2.Get a workflow template;

You will learn how to create your own workflow template later, but now, please get one of the example templates after login by click "Examples" in the menu. Here, please click the "Task 1-2-3" example. The example template will be put into your template library, check what you already have in your template library by click "Template Library" in the menu.

3.Start a workflow process;

From the Template Library, click "Start" after "Task123", you will then be asked to give it an instance name, name it as "mytask123", then click "Start" button. The workflow process will be initiated.

4.Check work list;

Click "Work list" in the menu, and you will see a process which is named as "mytask123", and the Task name is "Step 1". Click on "Step 1" to continue;

5.Do task;

The detail of "Step 1" task will be displayed. Following the instruction to do your work, then click "Done" button to complete this task. 6.Repeat step 4 to 5 until the workflow process end. Go to your work list again, you will see a task named "Step 2", go to its detail page, and click "Done" button to complete task "Step 2". Go to your work list for the third time, you will see a task named "Step 3", complete it. Now, go to your work list, you will see there is no tasks in process "mytask123" left.
