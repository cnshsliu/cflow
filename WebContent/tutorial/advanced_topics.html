Advanced Topics

  1. Is MWF multi-tenant?
    Yes, MWF is multi-tenant;
  2. How MWF manage multi-tenant?
    • MWF use organization (ORG) to manage tenant
    • A user can create and belong to one to many ORGs,
    • A user can see other users (peers) who belong to any ORG the user belongs to.
    • A process started with a user (the starter) can dispatch tasks to starter’s peers.

  3. The relationship among workflow role, team and process doers.
    • A workflow TASK can be assigned to one to many users, or one to many roles.
    • A team defines the mapping between role and user(s)
    • The role will be mapped to users at process runtime, by matching role to user(s) defined in team
